About Me

I am a Licensed Electrical Engineer and Registered Master Electrician with experience in engineering instruction, leadership, and operations coordination. I have worked as a college instructor teaching electrical engineering, mathematics, and basic electronics, where I facilitated hands-on circuit building, soldering, testing, and technical evaluation activities.

Beyond academics, I also gained experience in supervision and data management through my work with the Philippine Statistics Authority and as an online grader for business and advanced Excel applications. I am passionate about continuous learning, technology, and leadership, which also led me to serve for seven years in youth leadership and event coordination roles. Currently, I am expanding my skills in web and computer programming while building projects that combine technical knowledge, creativity, and problem-solving.
